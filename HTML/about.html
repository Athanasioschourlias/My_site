My Life

My full name, as you may have already guessed, is Athanasios Chourlias and I’m currently studying Computer Science, at Harokopeio University of Athens. That makes me feel lucky, as not as many people as you might think are able to study and work with what they are passionate about. You ask where that passion comes from? Well, I was always a curious person, and one thing that caught my attention the most, was electronic devices, especially electronic computers and what goes on “behind the scenes” or for lack of a better word “behind the screen”. That curiosity ended up with me reading and learning every day more and more about programming and software development. That was when I got my first Arduino and started experimenting with it. At the time, little I knew that I was actually doing was learning my first “programming” language. All these little projects brought me joy and made me feel like I do something meaningful, so I kept experimenting, failing hard, learning and trying again and again. As the years went by, I kept learning new stuff, such as the different fields in Computers Science and the different types of software development, and I kept on seeking more information for the ones I found the most interesting. Now, after all these years, and all the experience I gained, through research and mini experiments, I believe I have a clearer view of what “path” I want to follow. That is to get to know more about A.I research and development and create algorithms.
